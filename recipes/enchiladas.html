Green Chicken Enchiladas.

Creamy chicken enchiladas that are always a hit with family gatherins! Also great with left-over turkey meat from Thanksgiving.

Ingredients

  • One Pound of White Chicken Breast or Turkey
  • Cream Chese
  • One Can of Corn
  • One Can of Beans (Black or Pinto)
  • Two Cups of Green Enchilada Sauce
  • One cup of Taco Blend Cheese
  • Flour Tortillas

Optinal

  • One Jalapeno
  • A Spring of Cilantro
  • Taco Seasoning

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Shred chicken or turkey.
  3. Melt cream cheese with shredded chicken, half a cup of green sauce, a can of corn and a can of beans (Pinto or Black). Add optinal taco seasoning to the mixture if desired.
  4. Lay an even bed of green sauce
    5. on the bottom of a glass baking dish.
  5. Roll the mixture in tortillas and lay in the dish.
  6. Pour remaining green sauce over rolled tortillas.
  7. Sprinkle taco blend cheese on top.
  8. Bake dish for 30 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
  9. Garnish with jalapeno and cilantro.
