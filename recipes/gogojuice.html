GoGo Juice.

Is this a Jojo refrence? No, it's actually a sweet drink that can be spiked with alcohol if desireded or blended into a sweet treat in the summer!

Ingredients

  • One Gallon of Apple Juice
  • Ginger Ale
  • One Packet of Fruit Punch Flavored Kool Aid

Optional

  • One Lime

Steps

  1. Mix the Kool Aid with the gallon of apple juice.
  2. Pour the Ginger ale over the mixture
  3. Surve cold with ice and optinal Lime slice.
  4. You thought it was Honey BooBoo, but it was actually Dio!
